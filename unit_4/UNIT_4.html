About

Weaving is an art, an expression of our time, which can have the brilliance of patining, the dimension of sculture, the shape of invention, and the form of imagination Else Regensteiner

Breanna is a self taught fibre artist based out of her home in Sydney, Australia. After starting loom weaving as a hobby in 2017, Breanna turned her love for fibre art into a small business while continuing to work full time in marketing. Through her work, Breanna hopes to add warmth in any room and to create an inspiring environment with each handmade peice she weaves.
