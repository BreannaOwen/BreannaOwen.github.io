About

Weaving is an art, an expression of our time, which can have the brilliance of patining, the dimension of sculture, the shape of invention, and the form of imagination Else Regensteiner

Breanna is a self taught fibre artist based out of her home in Sydney, Australia. With no prior experience in weaving, knitting or crocheting, the start of a new hobby began after taking a loom weaving class in 2017.

From buying baskets of wool to weaving a range of looms on a cardboard frame, Breanna turned her love for fibre art into a small business while continuing to work full time in marketing.

Contact

Breanna hopes to add warmth to any room with her wool and cotton blend wall hangings, and works to create an inspiring environment with each handmade piece she weaves.

If you would like a custom made wall hanging, or have a question about the art, contact Breanna by submitting the contact form. Or follow her on social media to keep updated with the creation process.
